Our Project Management and Building Construction

We have conquered the skill of of Project Management to an advanced level. We specialize in management of multiple complex projects in the same time. We implement Scrum Methodology, a subset of Agile framework, which has proven to be the best model for handling most complicated and huge projects with very ease. Parallel Scheduling, generation of Project Budget, keeping up with regular and daily Progress Meetings, Site Program Plan, Consultant Management, Contract Administration, Cash Flow Projections, Optimization of Cost and Time, Generation of regular Progress Reports, Quality Control, on time Vendor Payment Approvals are some of our everyday tasks.

Our team's Strong Design and Construction Skills meet to solving most complex problems in real time construction, like enhancement of structural strength to disaster landing. Repair & Retrofitting of aged old buildings into a classic brand new structure with full strength like a fresh construction with enhanced energy efficiency (or) short working skill is our specialty.
